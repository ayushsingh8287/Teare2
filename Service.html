Hospital Management System

A Hospital Management System is a software system that helps in handling, tracking, and automating various hospital functions.

Patient Registration

  • OPD Registration
  • Consultant Appointment Management
  • OPD Billing

OPD Consultation

  • Patient Data Record Keeping
  • Prescription Printing
  • Requesting Investigations
  • Referring to Other Doctors

Issue Certificates & Forms

  • Medical Certificate for Sickness
  • Fitness Certificate
  • Referral Letter
  • Hospital Admit Letter

Hospital Admission

  • Admit Patient
  • Allocate Bed - ICU, NICU, Deluxe
  • Create Admission Summary
  • Assign Doctor

Hospital Stay

  • Printing Consent Forms
  • Daily Treatment Records
  • Daily Vital Monitoring
  • Surgery Records

Hospital Discharge

  • Discharge Patient & Print Summary
  • Generate Final Bill
  • Send Follow-Up Reminders

System Configuration & Reports

  • Create Consultant Accounts
  • Create Staff Accounts
  • Configure Services & Rates
  • View Reports (Admitted, Discharged, etc.)
